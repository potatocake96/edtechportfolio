Privacy, Terms & Data

Our commitment to privacy, data security, and responsible use of technology in schools.

Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

Simon Dass EdTech Consultancy is committed to protecting the privacy of students, teachers, and school staff. We understand that educational data is sensitive and must be handled with the utmost care and respect.

Information We Collect

When you contact us or use our services, we may collect:

  • Contact information (name, email, school, role)
  • Information about your school's needs and requirements
  • Usage data for tools and services (with appropriate consent)

We do not collect student personal information unless explicitly required for a specific service, and only with appropriate consent and data protection measures in place.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide consulting services and respond to inquiries
  • Develop and improve tools and services
  • Communicate about services, updates, and relevant information
  • Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • With explicit consent from the data subject
  • To trusted service providers who assist in our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at simon.dass.1996@gmail.com.

Data & Security

Data Minimisation

We follow the principle of data minimisation: we only collect and process data that is necessary for the specific purpose. We do not collect more information than needed, and we delete data when it is no longer required.

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure hosting and infrastructure
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Staff training on data protection

Student Data

When working with student data:

  • We comply with all applicable privacy laws and regulations
  • We require explicit consent and data sharing agreements
  • We use de-identified or aggregated data where possible
  • We do not use student data for commercial purposes
  • We support schools in maintaining control over their data

Consulting and professional learning are delivered by a Victorian Institute of Teaching (VIT) registered teacher (Registration no. 615660).

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected parties and relevant authorities in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.
