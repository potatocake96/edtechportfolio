Systems designed with schools

Examples of internal systems developed as part of consultancy work. Each implementation is tailored to a school's context and hosted in school‑controlled environments where possible.

Intervention & Adjustment Systems

Systems that support learning adjustment documentation, intervention tracking, and NCCD evidence—built around teacher workflows and school‑owned data models.

  • Standardised adjustment commentary and evidence capture
  • Structured intervention tracking over time
  • Workflows that reduce administrative load for teachers
  • Designed to align with school privacy and governance expectations

Reporting & Leadership Dashboards

Dashboards that surface attendance, wellbeing, intervention and support information for leadership teams, while keeping sensitive detail within school systems.

  • Multi‑school or multi‑campus visibility where required
  • Meeting‑ready summaries and longitudinal views
  • Dashboards designed around real leadership questions
  • Built to sit on top of school‑controlled data environments

Teacher Workflow Systems

Internal tools that support classroom evidence capture, planning, and documentation, with a focus on complex learners and alternative pathways.

  • Evidence and artefact capture for day‑to‑day learning
  • Workflows that support competency‑based and skills‑focused programs
  • Support for flexible and alternative curriculum pathways
  • Designed collaboratively with teachers and support staff
