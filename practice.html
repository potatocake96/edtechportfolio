The practice

Learning Systems Studio is a specialist practice focused on designing practical, privacy-conscious digital systems for schools.

What Learning Systems Studio is

An education systems consultancy that designs internal systems for schools—learning support, intervention tracking, workflows, and leadership visibility—rather than selling off‑the‑shelf software. Each engagement is built around the realities of a specific school: existing platforms, staff capacity, and the needs of complex learners.

Who we work with

Schools supporting complex learners

Specialist settings, alternative and flexible learning environments, and schools working with disengaged or complex cohorts.

Leadership teams

Principals, assistant principals and inclusion leaders who need clearer visibility of adjustments, interventions and support.

IT & data teams

School and system teams who want strong digital systems without surrendering control of student data to external vendors.

Systems design, not generic platforms

Many schools are surrounded by platforms. The real gap is coherent internal systems: how information flows, how support is tracked, and how staff make decisions. Learning Systems Studio focuses on internal systems design—workflows, data models, and tooling that sit inside school‑controlled environments.
