AI in education consulting services

Engagement Options

Schools typically engage Simon on a flexible consultancy basis, similar to bringing in a specialist educator or consultant to design and implement systems that support teaching teams and leadership.

This allows schools to pilot ideas, improve existing systems, or receive ongoing support without committing to large software contracts.

Consultation Session

A focused strategic conversation with school leadership or staff to discuss challenges related to learning adjustments, intervention tracking, teacher workflows, or digital systems.

Example inclusions:

  • • reviewing existing systems
  • • identifying workflow challenges
  • • discussing implementation strategies
  • • planning potential pilots or improvements

Pricing: From $250–$400 per session

Specialist Implementation Day

A full-day engagement where Simon works with a school on-site or remotely to design, implement, or refine systems that support teachers and leadership teams.

Example activities:

  • • designing intervention or adjustment tracking systems
  • • building teacher workflow tools
  • • developing leadership dashboards
  • • implementing digital learning support systems
  • • staff training and professional learning

Pricing: Typically $900–$1,500 per day

This engagement model is similar to hiring a specialist CRT or consultant for targeted system development and training.

Pilot Implementation or Short Project

For schools wishing to trial or implement a structured system across a program, team, or cohort.

Example inclusions:

  • • system design and configuration
  • • pilot rollout with staff
  • • workflow refinement
  • • feedback and iteration

Pricing: Custom pricing depending on scope

Retained Systems Specialist

For schools wanting ongoing support designing, maintaining, and evolving digital systems that support teaching teams and leadership. This model allows schools to retain Simon on a regular CRT-style basis to provide continuous support and improvement.

Typical activities:

  • • maintaining learning support or intervention tracking systems
  • • refining dashboards and reporting tools
  • • assisting staff with workflows and documentation
  • • developing or adapting internal tools
  • • staff training and professional learning

Example engagement formats:

  • • 1 day per week
  • • 2 days per fortnight
  • • several days per month

Pricing: Available as a monthly retainer or day‑rate engagement
