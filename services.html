AI in education consulting services

Strategic AI integration for schools—focused on teacher workload reduction, inclusive practice, and curriculum-aligned digital tools.

Technology in schools should make teachers' lives easier. I design and implement tools that respect teacher expertise, reduce admin burden, and improve student outcomes.

My Approach

What's Included
